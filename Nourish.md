**Nourish Guide**  
**Milk Dues**

1. **Know the Need**  
Milk helps grow. Feed well.  
2. **Time to Feed**  
Give when baby cries.  
3. **Give All**  
Let milk flow long.  
4. **Clean All**  
Wash hands, clean skin.  
5. **Space to Rest**  
Sit soft, feel calm.  
6. **Light, Low**  
Dim lights, rest eyes.  
7. **Calm the Mind**  
Stay still, no stress.  
8. **Hold Close**  
Baby near, warmth felt.  
9. **Keep Full**  
Feed till full—pause, check.  
10. **Thank Life**  
Milk’s gift, so give
