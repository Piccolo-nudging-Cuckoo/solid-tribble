### **Singing Guide**  
**Songs for Joy**

1. **Hear the Sound**  
Start soft, hum low.

2. **Feel the Beat**  
Tap toes, clap hands.

3. **Small Songs First**  
Sing one note, hold strong.

4. **Match the Tone**  
Stay with the sound, tune close.

5. **Move in Time**  
Swing slow, dance free.

6. **Use Soft Words**  
Sing kind, sing true.

7. **Make Space Clear**  
Open room, songs rise.

8. **Breathe Deep**  
Take breath, feel calm.

9. **Share Each Voice**  
All sounds, one song.

10. **Thank the Song**  
Sing loud, love near.
